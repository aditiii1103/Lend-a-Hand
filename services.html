Who we are

We are a bunch of graduates from ABC college who while pursuing the B.tech degree established this organization. This led to the establishment of LAH. We brought together several other students , faculties and college management team for helping the less abled ones.

Services

Lend a Hand (LAH)is a nonprofit organization, started by a group of individuals engaged in creating values in the lives of needy & poor people, by supporting financially for diversified needs, by knowledge empowerment and by assisting them in building up a bright future. The organization has been founded with the core principles of assisting the destitutes by providing basic needs & services as an upliftment and also making difference in the lives of under-privileged people by giving best support to fulfill their dream and needs.

Apart from this, they have multiple projects that involve donating toys to children, blood to patients, medical assistance in villages, basic furniture pieces to government schools, and aid to special students like those who have lost their eyesight and hearing.


Education

We run a one-year programme to facilitate school readiness for children from low-income communities aged 4-6 years. The idea is to build a strong foundation in language and social skills in a safe, secure, and happy learning environment. After kindergarten, the NGO also continues to support them through their school years and until they become employable.

Health Care Facilities

Currently, we are working on various projects in 17 different states across India involving healthcare professionals, disaster management practitioners, social workers, etc. It reaches out to segments affected by extreme poverty, disasters, neglect and years of injustice. Their major focus is to address the healthcare and nutritional needs of the affected population. They also work to improve sexual and reproductive health, maternal, infant and child health in emergencies and have vast experience working in disaster-prone areas.

Vocational Skill Development Training

ANK has been running vocational skill development programs since 2006 to bridge this gap. It has covered a wide gamut of learning for youth. From Basic Computer and English to 2D and 3D Animation, Retail and Hospitality Services, ANK has experience of running a wide array of vocational skill development programs. These industry designed courses have wide acceptability among recruiters and also provide skills of basic entrepreneurship to the trainees. Every year hundreds of youths are being churned at these vocational training centres which are capable of generating employability for these underprivileged youth. These are our steps to success.

About us

Our Organization "LEND A HAND" is public welfare non-government organization that functions independently. It is a non-profit organization. It was established in 2020 during the Covid times when thousands of people were in the dire need of help and assistance. We continue to provide help to various people mainly for there three basic needs that are food/water, homes and clothes. We have built trust of hundereds people who were very generous to extend there hand and help the poeple in need.
