Purpose

We are trying to help the people in the three most basic aspect to life that are : food , shelter, clothing .Every person on earth deserves these three things for living a normal life . We have become the most trusted organization through which number of people are able to serve others. We make use of modern facilities and well developed procedure so that we keep your trust in us high and give complete transperency to our work.

Food

Food is the most basic necessity of life. It is responsible for physical and mental growth and development of ones body. It provides us nutrients that provide: energy for activity, growth, and all functions of the body such as breathing, digesting food, and keeping warm.

So we help the people who can't arrange for themselves 2 meals a day. We also provide them eatables and other groceries.

Shelter

Shelter is a basic human need crucial for survival in cases of natural hazards or conflict. It provides security, personal safety and protection from the weather, and prevents ill health and disease. Adequate housing provides people with dignity and the opportunity to lead a normal life.

We provide basic building material and financial assitance to people who are deprived of homes.

Clothes

Clothing provides physical safeguards to the body. Clothes can be worn for status, modesty, adornment and identification. Clothes protect us from occupational hazards, weather, heat, dust, rain and cold.We need clothes to cover ourselves and protect us from Dust, rain, insects, sunlight, etc.

We provide people with normal as well as winter clothing so they can protect themselves from the cruel weather conditions.

About us

Our Organization "LEND A HAND" is public welfare non-government organization that functions independently. It is a non-profit organization. It was established in 2020 during the Covid times when thousands of people were in the dire need of help and assistance. We continue to provide help to various people mainly for there three basic needs that are food/water, homes and clothes.
